i-Vet was created to streamline veterinary care across the Municipality of Orani. This centralized web system simplifies clinic operations from appointment scheduling to patient records allowing vets to focus more on caring for animals. With i-Vet, clinics can offer faster, more organized service, strengthening the connection between pet owners and the care their animals deserve.

About Me

Hi! I'm Joemari D. Roberto

A recent graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, and I’m excited to dive into the world of graphic design. With a strong foundation in design software like Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, I’m passionate about bringing ideas to life through branding, logo creation, and visual storytelling. My goal is to blend creativity with technical skills to design engaging and memorable visuals. I’m always eager to learn, adapt, and grow in the fast-evolving field of design. Looking forward to creating impactful work and connecting with others in the creative industry!

Here’s some more information about me:

  • Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, and Canva.
  • Passionate about minimalism and modern design trends.

Feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate or just say hi!
